SoftPack

Software Packaging platform for reproducible research

SoftPack Tutorial - 5min ⏱️

Multiuser cross-platform environments

Share software environments with your team across campus or across the globe. Deploy on any Linux distribution on your local network, in the cloud or even on your laptop.

Extensive support for Python & R

Builtin support for thousands of packages in Python & R with the option to add virtually any software written in any language, even with custom toolchains.

Intuitive Graphical Interface

Easy to use graphical interface for managing your environments. Add, remove or update packages from an environment and monitor the progress in real-time as your environment is built.
